Results of the "Who the Fuck is Xûr? Quiz"


Question 1:


Xûr is the Agent of the Nine. Who are the Nine? Only time will tell...

Question 2:


The Nine originating in Corona-Borealis Supercluster is the most re-told tale in Destiny lore regarding their origins. Lore on the matter goes back to the Destiny Grimoire entry for the Nine and can be found more recently on the lore tabs of Trials of the Nine gear. The Emissary of the Nine also mentions this location to Guardians who have gone Flawless in Trials when they visit her atop the Spire.

Question 3:


He appeared in the hallway on the way to the Vanguard, directly beside the Crucible Quartermaster.

Question 4:


The common misconception is that he carried Sunbreakers more than any other exotic in Year One, but in reality, it was Voidfang Vestments that came up for sale the most frequently.

Question 5:


What?? Seriously? Why would anyone waste their Strange Coins on an exotic heavy weapon? Especially a Rocket Launcher! I need to get those Sunbreakers, because they'll never come back!

Question 6:


While the engrams are named "Isochronal" (meaning "equal time") the shape of all engrams is a regular, 12-sided pentagonal prism — better known as a dodecahedron, just like a 12-sided die for tabletop gaming!

Question 7:


Items Xûr has sold in Destiny 2 have cost 23, 29, 31, and 97 legendary shards — all prime numbers.

Question 8:


This symbol glowed on the left side of the planter directly to the right of the Tower spawn point when Xûr was present.

Question 9:


The discussion that Orin had with these Guardianless Ghosts can be found on the lore tab of the Trials of the Nine sniper rifle, "The Long Walk". These nine Ghosts are one potential set of candidates for the mysterious group known as "The Nine" for whom Xûr works.

Question 10:


If you stand and listen to Xûr, he can eventually be overheard to say, "Your traveler has a dark mirror." Perhaps he's referring to the "Triangle Ships" Mara Sov attempted to warn us about this week! He can also be overheard to say, "Something inside wishes to connect", but this is taken to be in reference to something inside him, not the Traveler.
